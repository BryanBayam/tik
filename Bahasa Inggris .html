PERSONAL LETTER

Personal letter is a letter that is used for private purpose, and content associated with a private matter between a person with another person.

The part of a personal letter:

  1. Postal address (of the sender)
  2. place, date, month, year letter writing (the time when the letter is written)
  3. Greeting/salutation (it usually started with "Dear" then followed by the recipient name and is ended with coma)
  4. The opening sentence (sometimes, it can refer to a previous letter)
  5. Contents/body of the letter (the main part of the letter. It includes the message that the writer wants to tell the recipient. It can be divided into paragraphs)
  6. The concluding sentence/ closure (it indicates the letter is going to end. The writer may ask the recipient to reply here)
  7. Closing Greetings (it includes a few words on one line. It end with coma, such as: yours sincerely, best regards, love etc.
  8. Signature (of the writer)
  9. Name of the sender
